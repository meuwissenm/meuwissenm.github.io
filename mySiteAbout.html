    Cooking and coding

    My other passion aside from coding is cooking. I think coding and cooking have a lot of similarities. Understanding basic methods and adapting them to different recipes I learn. Coordinating the stovetop, the kitchen equipment, and the oven trying to figure out when I should start cooking the different parts of my meal and what resources are going to to be used.

    When I become familiar with my favorite recipes, I begin to understand them well enough that I can change my cooking methods to fit better with the meal I want and perfect each process.

    And I also learned cleaning up while cooking frees up resources and makes it easier to clean up later.

    Cooking and coding both take a lot of forethought and logic to coordinate the different moving parts and have the result turn out as planned.
